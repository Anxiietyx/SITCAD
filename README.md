# SabahSprout (SITCAD)

AI-powered kindergarten education management and learning system.

## 🚀 Project Overview
SabahSprout is a comprehensive AI-driven EdTech platform that transforms early childhood education administration and delivery in Malaysian kindergartens. The system is purpose-built for the Sabah region and aligns with the Malaysian national preschool curriculum (**DSKP KSPK Semakan 2026**).

Our mission is to empower educators with intelligent tools that streamline administrative workflows, enable data-driven instructional decisions, and support holistic child development tracking. By leveraging artificial intelligence and curriculum-aligned pedagogical insights, SabahSprout enables teachers to focus more on meaningful student interactions while ensuring structured, evidence-based educational progression.

### Targeted Users
- **Teachers**: Manage classrooms, track student progress, and generate AI-assisted lesson plans.
- **Parents**: Monitor their child's development and communicate with teachers.
- **Admins**: Manage users and view system-wide analytics.

## ✨ Core Features
- **AI-Powered Lesson Planning**: Generate intelligent, curriculum-aligned lesson plans with personalized suggestions based on student progress and learning styles.
- **Comprehensive Progress Tracking**: Real-time monitoring of student development across all pedagogical domains with detailed analytics and growth visualizations.
- **Automated Reporting**: Generate administrative and parent reports automatically, reducing manual documentation burden for educators.
- **Early Intervention System**: Identify at-risk learners early through AI analysis and provide targeted intervention recommendations.
- **Teacher-Parent Communication**: Unified messaging platform enabling seamless collaboration and information sharing about student progress.
- **Administrative Dashboard**: System-wide analytics, user management, and operational insights for school administrators.
- **Curriculum-Aligned Assessment**: Assessments mapped to the six domains of the Malaysian preschool curriculum with evidence-based learning outcomes.
- **Multi-Role Access Control**: Role-based dashboards and features tailored for teachers, parents, and administrators.

## 📖 Domain Logic: DSKP KSPK Revision 2026
The system integrates pedagogical suggestions based on:
- Tunjang Komunikasi
- Tunjang Kerohanian, Sikap dan Nilai
- Tunjang Kemanusiaan
- Tunjang Sains dan Teknologi
- Tunjang Fizikal dan Estetika
- Tunjang Keterampilan Diri
